Our story stats with a farmer from Sonoma County, California named Patrick. 
His parents and their parents before them were all farmers. 
They grew grapes for wine makers in the area, but they had one personel crop that they kept as a family tradition. 
They grew gravenstein apples in a small orchard, one of the last in the U.S. 
Now, Patrick works to keep up the recipes of his family and keep the trees healthy, but after
bring one of his family's famous apple pies to a bake sale, some have taken an interest in what makes his pies so good.
